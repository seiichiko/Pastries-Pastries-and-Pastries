Pastries, Pastries, and Pastries! Onlne Store

This is a Java-based application designed to streamline pastry shop operations by allowing customers to place orders and enabling authorized staff to manage inventory stock through a secure staff password.

Primary Functions:
- Customers can browse pastries and place orders within the application.
- Staff members can access an inventory management panel—secured with a staff password—to update stock levels and maintain accurate product availability.

Usage:
- Compile and run the application from your IDE or terminal.
- Customers can view the menu and place orders.
- Staff can enter the staff password to open the inventory panel and update stock quantities.
